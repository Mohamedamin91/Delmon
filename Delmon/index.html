WelcomeBusiness information an organisation

We are a multi dimensional entity with diverse business interests. We are focusing on successful future for our clients, employees and owners by leading our industry in quality and value of service. Delmon Group of Companies is one of the major emergent manufacturing, engineering, trading and marketing group with its Head Quarters strategically based at the Port and Oil City of Eastern Province, Dammam in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The group has diverse business sectors and is pioneer in various business ventures in the region

Delmon has attained new heights, undergoing a radical transformation since its inception in 1981. The group is a part of the AI-Ghunaim Brothers Group and since then, it has had an impressive record of undertaking major contracts and supplies to various segments in the market.

Quality of work and customer satisfaction has been the corner stones of our corporate policy and we sincerely try to achieve utmost perfection in the services we provide. The company is managed by a team of professionals in different disciplines.
